Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep time: 10 mins
Yield: 4

Ingredients

4 cup butter, softened
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
8 Italian bread slices
4 (3/4-oz.) provolone cheese slices
4 (3/4-oz.) mozzarella cheese slices

Steps

  1. Stir together butter and Parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slice. Place 4 bread slices, buttered sides down, on wax paper. Top with provolone and mozzarella cheeses; top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides up.
  3. Cook sandwiches, in batches, on a hot griddle or in a non-stick skillet over medium heat, gently pressing with a spatlia, 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
